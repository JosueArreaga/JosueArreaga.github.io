Methods to Facilitate Calculus

Premise

Nationwide between 25% to 30% of students who take Calculus for the first time fail. Even failing rates of 40% in a class are not unusual. Hence, is there a better way to learn Calculus? Could our books be wrong, not conceptually but on how they unfold? Could we simplify the data into a set of concepts and reinforce them to facilitate Calculus?

My Contribution

For this project, I decompressed the idea of limits, derivatives, and integrals into three separate booklets to help aid the conceptual learning of Calculus one. These booklets are now available for students at Miami Dade College at no cost!
